FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school admission requirements?

Admission requirements include birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), residence proof, and a completed application form.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply by filling out the online form or visiting our school office. Remember to submit all required documents.

What is the fee structure?

The fee structure varies by grade. Please visit our Fee Structure page or contact the office for detailed information.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer transportation for most areas. Kindly check the transportation route details and fees.
