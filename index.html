Our Curriculum

CBC Model
CBC(Competency-Based Curriculum) is a learner-centered model of education that focuses on developing learnersâ€™ knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes (competencies) that are essential for their personal development, lifelong learning, and contribution to society.
8:4:4 System
The 8-4-4 system was introduced in 1985 by the Kenyan government to align education with the country's socio-economic development goals. The structure stands for:8 years of Primary School,4 years of Secondary School &4 years of University/College Education.
Technical courses
Skilled courses are short or long-term programs that focus on practical, hands-on training to equip learners with specific skills and competencies for particular jobs or industries. They prepare individuals for technical, vocational, or specialized careers rather than academic or theoretical roles.

Scholarship

A Financial award or grant given to students to help cover the costs of their education.Typically awarded based on academic excellence, talents, skills, or specific needs (like financial hardship).

Providing Necessities

Are the basic items and resources that students need to fully participate in learning activities, both in the classroom and during homework or study time. These include materials for writing, reading, studying etc.

Utility Bills

Utility bills are monthly payments made to cover the costs of essential services that help us function smoothly. These services are usually provided by utility companies. eg water & electricity bills

Equipping the laboratory

Furnishing a laboratory with the necessary tools, instruments, and materials needed for practical experiments and learning activities in science, technology, engineering, and related fields.

Supporting Technical Skills
